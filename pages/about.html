Mark Cunningham spent over fifteen years as a Creative Executive at Ralph Lauren, before starting his own design firm in 2005. Since then he has worked on a wide range of projects, including residential interiors, hotel design, architectural consulting and commercial concepts.

His work incorporates found pieces from around the world, furniture of his own design and fine artwork to express the unique style and personality of his clients.
